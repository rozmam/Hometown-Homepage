Visit Vinica

Enjoy the irresistible beauty of untouched nature. Whether you’re looking for a place filled with peace and quiet, an active vacation, an unforgettable adventure or a unique experience, you’ve come to the right place. Explore the fascinating scenery, breathe in the fresh air, visit beguiling castles, stay in magical houses, and taste the unique and authentic dishes inspired by nature itself.

Top three activities to do in Vinica

Swiming in Kolpa river

The Kolpa river is among cleanliness and warmest river in Slovenia and Croatia (border river between SLO and CRO). It's perfect for summer refreshment and recreation, offering opportunities for swimming and other water activities.

Hiking in beutiful nature

Go for a walk in the untouched nature. The beautiful trails through the lovely forests by the Kolpa river offer some amazing views of the river.

Trip to the sea

The stunning Adriatic Sea is just a 50-minute drive away from Vinica. We can definetely make use of that for a day trip.
